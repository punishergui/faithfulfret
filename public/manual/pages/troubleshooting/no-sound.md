# No Sound Troubleshooting

If your amp powers on but no guitar sound is heard, run this list in order.

:::warning
Turn volume down before unplugging cables.
:::

## Quick checks

- Confirm guitar volume knob is up.
- Reseat instrument cable on both ends.
- Try a known-good cable.
- Switch to a clean preset.
- Raise post volume slowly.

## If still silent

1. Test with another guitar.
2. Test with headphones (if available).
3. Factory reset only after backing up key settings.
